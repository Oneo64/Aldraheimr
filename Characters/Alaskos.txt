Alaskös Ereviyne

Male
Age: 43, looks mid thirties
Alignment: Neutral

Homeland:  Minbaslûind, Esrelu
Race:      Cold Elf

Daughter: Zelessie



[[DESCRIPTION]]
Alaskös is a Cold Elf from Minbaslûind. He has silvery white hair, grey pupiless eyes, smooth pale skin, and long earlobes. He wears a grey cloak, and a dark red tunic. He has a belt, and he wears fur boots. He is 173 (5'8) centimeters tall.

He carries a flanged mace, and a kite shield painted grey with him.

He works as a guard. Usually during the day he can be seen patrolling the streets of Minbaslûind.



[[PERSONALITY]]
Alaskös acts calm, and sometimes act excessively elegant or regal.



[[ABILITIES]]
Alaskös has 25 years of experience as a guard. He is skilled with the weaponry he has, making him one of the more valued fighters in Minbaslûind. As a cold elf he speaks Esrel as his native tongue, but that is the only language he knows. He also has skill in stealth, and is able to quietly move if he wants.
