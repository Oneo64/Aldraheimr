[Year 953]
Meyrúna is born.


[Year 960]
Þone decides to teach Meyrúna herbology. Some of the plants learned were ekraldys toadstools, which were poisonous unless they were fermented.


[Year 965]
(Feburary)
Njall decided to buy a lyre. He gifted it to Meyrúna.

(March)
Meyrúna takes interest in woodcarving, passed down from her father. First thing she carved was a fork.


[Year 972]
(September)
There was a centaur raid, coming from the neighboring kingdom Vatakond. They took shelter in the community halls.
