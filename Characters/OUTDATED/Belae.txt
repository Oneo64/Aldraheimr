[[DESCRIPTION]]
Beläe is a cold elf from Vesesvala. She has green pupiless eyes, smooth pale skin, and blonde hair. Like all cold elves, her earlobes are long. She wears a tan triangular fur cap, long dark aqua tunic, a belt, and a pair of shoes.
